# Neuralchemy Labs

Neuralchemy Labs is the experimental arm of Neuralchemy.  
This organization focuses on rapid prototyping, technical exploration, and early-stage ideas.

---

## Projects

[![Poly-Reasoner](https://img.shields.io/badge/Poly--Reasoner-View-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/Neural-alchemy-Labs/poly-reasoner)  
Multi-agent system that evaluates ideas from independent perspectives and exposes trade-offs.

[![Orbitcode](https://img.shields.io/badge/Orbitcode-View-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/Neural-alchemy-Labs/orbitcode)  
Offline-first coding assistant focused on privacy and local control.

[![Unbuilt Ideas](https://img.shields.io/badge/Unbuilt--Ideas-View-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/Neural-alchemy-Labs/unbuilt-ideas)

Natural language stock screener chatbot.

---

## Purpose

Neuralchemy Labs exists to:
- Explore new technical directions  
- Prototype ideas quickly  
- Test assumptions early  
- Learn in public  

Not every experiment becomes a product.  
The goal is discovery and iteration.
